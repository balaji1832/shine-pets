The Science of Floating

Hydrotherapy and swimming provide low-impact benefits that ground-based exercise simply can’t match.

Joint Health

80% buoyancy reduces stress on joints, making it ideal for senior pets and those recovering from surgery.

Recreational Play

Mental stimulation and pure canine joy—exercise in a safe, controlled environment.

Weight Management

Swimming burns calories up to 5x the rate of walking, providing a high-efficiency workout that keeps your pet lean and heart-healthy.

Stress Relief

The rhythmic movement and warmth of the water have a natural calming effect on anxious dogs.

Always Warm

Heated to a perfect 85°F (29°C) to prevent muscle stiffness.

Pristine Aquatic Sanctuary

UV Filtration System

State-of-the-art dual UV and ozone filtration keeps the water pristine without heavy chemicals, protecting sensitive skin and coats.

Non-Slip Ramp Entry

Aero-entry ramps with specialized grip surfaces allow even the most nervous swimmers to enter at their own pace.

Post-Swim Spa Room

Every session includes a warm rinse and professional velocity drying to ensure your pet leaves fluffy and fresh.
